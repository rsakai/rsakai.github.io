About

Ryo Sakai - PhD., PDEng., MSc., MSc.BMC., BSc.

  • 2006-2008

    Biomedical Communications

    After completing a BSc in Biological Chemistry at Bates College, Lewiston Maine, USA, Ryo started his first MSc in Biomedical Communications at the University of Toronto, Canada. During this program, he acquired graphical and visual communication skills as a medical illustrator.

  • 2008-2009

    Computing and Information Systems

    Ryo started his second MSc in Computing and Information Systems at Queen Mary, University of London, the UK to build a solid foundation in programming skills to tackle on data visualization projects and to build interactive visualization tools.

  • 2009-2011

    PDEng in User System Interaction

    Ryo pursued a position as a designer trainee in a post-Master professional engineering program at Eindhoven University of Technology in the Netherlands. During this period, he learned and practiced UX design principles and interned at Phillips Research to develop a mobile application for communication and collaboration among radiologists and radiographers.

  • 2011-2015

    PhD in Biological Data Visualization

    Then, Ryo joined the Visual Data Analysis group (led by Prof. Jan Aerts) at KU Leuven STADIUS/ESAT and started his doctoral study in Biological Data Visualization: Design and Analysis. During his studies, he has published several papers, received awards in visualization contests, and established collaborations with scientists and engineers all over the world.

  • 2016

    Freelance

    Ryo worked as a freelance data visualization specialist: a designer, training provider and researcher.

  • 2016-present

    Data Visualization Specialist

    Ryo is currently working as a Data Visualization Specialist for PharmiWeb Solutions, providing a wide range of service from general consultancy involving data analytics to implementation of data visualization tools for healthcare professionals and pharmaceutical companies.
